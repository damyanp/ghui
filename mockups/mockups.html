ghui — Toolbar / Table Separation Mockups

All mockups show the table filling the full application area (no margin). Each variant shows a different way to visually separate the toolbar from the table header row.

Option A: Accent Border Under Toolbar
A thin primary-colored (cyan) border at the bottom of the toolbar creates a clean, colorful dividing line. Simple and minimal — the toolbar "owns" the accent line.
